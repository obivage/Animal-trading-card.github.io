GOAT

A fascinating and adaptable domesticated animal, the goat has won the hearts of both farmers and animal lovers.

  • Scientific Name: Capra aegagrus hircus.
  • Habitat: Mountains, Grasslands, Deserts, Forests, Farmlands, Urban and Suburban Areas
  • Life-Span: 8 to 18 years
  • Production: Milk Production, Meat Production, Fiber production, Vegetation Management and Manure Production

Ultimately, goats are amazing animals that have a long history of interacting with people. Due to their unique hooves, they are renowned for their capacity to climb and balance on unstable surfaces.
